Why an account?

Read here without one. With one, you build your own.

AI News is free to read without signing in — every story on the front page is yours to browse, and every sentence the model writes still cites the source it came from. An account turns the same model into a tool you can point at the feeds you care about, instead of the default broadsheet.

01. Your feed, your sources

Add the RSS sources you actually read. Group them into lists. The model only summarizes what’s inside — nothing else creeps in.

02. A byline of your own

Publish your curated edition at /user-news/@you. Share the URL with anyone, or keep it private to yourself. Either way, every summary still links to the source feed it came from.

03. Mark what matters

Like articles you’d recommend. Save articles for later. Build a small archive of the pieces worth keeping — without leaving the page you’re reading.

04. Sourced, never guessed

Same rule as the public side: every sentence the model writes is anchored to a source citation. No invented quotes, no hidden attribution. An account doesn’t change that — it just puts you on the editor’s side of the page.
